Navigation

"Games For Sale" has relatively simple and responsive navigation, with every clickable link/image/pathway changing color, opacity or size when the cursor is hovering it.  The website also has a consistent header and footer for easy navigation between all pages on the site, to ensure users can never get too lost.  The "buy now" page is only accessible through the links on the homepage, and the consistent "preorder now" sidebar, present on every page to keep users up to date with upcoming games.  Furthermore, as is present on most websites, the header logo links back to the home page to make it easy and familiar for most users.
